How to Play?

The Wasps

Queen Wasp

The boss! Knock her out to win instantly.

Drone Wasp

Small but nimble wasps with low energy.

Worker Wasp

Steady workers with medium energy.

How to Win

  • Knock out the Queen Wasp for instant victory!
  • Knock out all wasps to clear the nest
  • Watch out! Wasps recover after 40 seconds

Click the Zap button to hit a random wasp!
